what is it?

twitter + tumblr = tweetlr. it crawls twitter for a given term, extracts photos out of the collected tweets and posts the images to tumblr. nice & clean & easy.

how does it look like in action?

to answer your question: anything goes. have a look at some examples:

  • coffeediary - for coffee lovers.
  • agile design camp 2011 - live barcamp coverage
  • digitalmediawomen - hamburgs finest digital media women
  • wirsounterwegs (german) - the original tweetlr!
  • feiereihoch3 - live party coverage.
  • hktbl - hackers gonna hack.
  • berlinonruby - conference live coverage.
  • biersounterwegs (german) - is it beer o'clock yet?
